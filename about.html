About us

The objectives of the TNPL 

1. To develop Tamil Nadu players to play competitive T20 matches 2. To provide a platform for Tamil Nadu players to exhibit their talent 3. To have sustainable model for developing infrastructure in districts, and 4. To bring in new audiences to the grounds.

The TNPL is a franchise-based T20 tournament comprising eight teams. The tender process for the Media Rights and Franchisee Rights was finalised in the Executive Committee Meeting held on April 20, 2016.
